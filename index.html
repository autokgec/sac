Lambhorgini Huracan

Lamborghini Huracan is a sports car manufactured by the Italian Sports Car giants Lamborghini. The Huracan was introduced to replace the company’s best selling car, Gallardo. The designation LP-610-4 indicates the 610 HP engine with 4 wheel drive and LP stands for Longitudinale Posteriure which indicates the position of the engine which is placed at the middle for greater balance. The engine is powered by both direct point fuel injection and multi point fuel injection and this is the first time that these two have been combined in a V10 engine, stamping the uniqueness that Lamborghini has tried to install in the car. The Huracán maintains the 5.2-litre naturally aspirated Audi or Lamborghini V10 engine from the Gallardo, tuned to generate a maximum power output of 449 kW. The car reaches a top speed of 341 kmph and gallops to 0-97 kmph in 2.5 seconds. The engine is economical at the same time with consuming just 12.5 litres enough to keep the beast going for 100 long kilometres. The car has an electronically controlled all wheel drive system and it aims to increase traction on various surfaces. The dual clutch 7 speed transmission with Lamborghini’s new Dual Clutch gear, the performance is better than ever. The Huracan comes with only automatic transmission. The suspension is also one of a kind with Lamborghini using magnetically controlled suspension. The magnetorheological suspension enables quick adjustment to the surface thus improving handling. With a road cost of 3.89 crores INR, the car is sure to burn a huge hole in anyone’s pocket but it is definitely worth it. Lamborghini tried to replace the Gallardo and it’s fare to say that they have done proper justice to the King of Italian Sportscars.
